Who we are

We are a group of people, from different fields and backgrounds, who care about the state of the world and its inhabitants. We recognize that there are likely billions of other people who do as well. We acknowledge that mankind's ability to improve the world is incredibly high, should a collective decision be made amongst ourselves. We believe there are enough people who want a better world, to make it a reality. We all have the power to exact change, when we work as a collective.

Why we're here

Articulating Science (“ArtSy” for short) is here to develop a hotline to society used for harnessing mass cooperation. To build a reputation as a source of clear and trustworthy information that is communicated throughout a myriad of communities, rapidly. The desired outcome is to improve our collective mental, physical, environmental and economic health. As they are all interlinked.

What we do

We use a combination of innovative and traditional communication methods to spread awareness about the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and invite collective action. Our approach inspires and facilitates collaboration among all the minds who want to contribute to solutions. We initiate and foster partnership between organisations, government, and the public, in order to meet all 17 SDGs.
